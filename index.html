Good questions

Before you ask.

The things people want to know before handing a project to someone they haven't met.

Do we need to get on a call?

No — that's the whole point. You pick a plan and shape your brief in a few taps, and everything after that lives in your dashboard: progress, updates, files and comments. You'll never have to sit through a status meeting.

How long does it take?

Launch is about 2 weeks, Signature about 4, and Studio about 8 — counted from the day your brief and deposit land, not from a kickoff call three weeks later.

How does paying work?

Every plan is 50% to start and 50% on launch, paid by card or PayPal. The moment your deposit clears, your project code is created and your dashboard goes live.

If I message you, how fast do you reply?

Within 1 business day — usually sooner. You message us right inside your dashboard, we reply in the same thread, and the whole conversation stays in one place instead of scattered across email.

What if I want changes?

Revisions are built into every plan: 1 round on Launch, 2 on Signature, and unlimited within scope on Studio. You leave your notes directly in your dashboard — no email threads to dig through.

What do you need from me?

Your brief — which is mostly tapping what fits — plus any logo, copy or images you already have. If you're not sure what you want yet, that's a normal place to start: say so in the brief and we'll shape it together.

What if my project isn't one of the plans?

Send a custom request. Tell us what you need — or what you're unsure about — and we'll review it and send you a quote. There's no payment until you approve it.

How do I keep track of everything?

With one code. Every project gets one (like OAK-2049) the moment it starts. Enter it at your dashboard from any device to see progress, updates, payments and files.

Still unsure? Send a custom request — it's free, and there's no commitment until you approve the quote.
